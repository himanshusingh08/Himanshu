Create premium editorial visuals for a fashion collection launch — driving engagement and building a consistent, aspirational brand identity across social media platforms.

Overview

Understanding
the challenge

Client

Anjum Khan is a fashion designer known for elegant, editorial-style collections. They needed a visual refresh for their social media presence to match the premium quality of their clothing line.

Objective

Create premium editorial visuals for the fashion launch that would elevate the brand's Instagram presence and drive significantly higher engagement rates.

Problem

The brand was struggling with low engagement rates and inconsistent visual identity across their social media channels, resulting in poor brand perception and missed conversion opportunities.

Process
01

Research

Studied the brand's tone, target audience demographics, competitor strategies, and current engagement patterns to identify key areas for improvement.

02

Concept

Created a soft editorial direction with warm, muted tones that reflect the premium nature of the brand while standing out in crowded social feeds.

03

Design

Designed a complete carousel series and story templates with consistent typography, color grading, and layout systems optimized for engagement.

04

Final Output

Delivered production-ready assets across all required formats — feed posts, carousels, stories, and highlight covers — with a comprehensive brand style guide.
