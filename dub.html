Case Study — Social + Campaign

DUB

Bold streetwear visual identity — raw energy meets sharp design in a brand language built for the streets.

IndustryStreetwear & Culture
ScopeSocial Media + Campaigns
RoleArt Direction & Design
Duration8 Weeks
The Challenge

Nomme needed a visual identity that embodied bold streetwear energy — loud, unapologetic, and instantly recognizable in every feed.

The existing brand presence felt too generic for the streetwear audience. Social media lacked consistency and the visual language didn't communicate the bold street credibility the brand deserved. We needed to bridge that gap without losing authenticity.

The Insight
Design should hit like a bass drop — instant, visceral, unforgettable. No subtlety, all impact.

Design Philosophy — DUB

Creative Direction

High-contrast boldness with unapologetic energy

We developed a visual system rooted in high-contrast blacks, electric reds, and typographic hierarchy. Every element serves a purpose — nothing is decorative, everything communicates.

The palette draws from natural materials: asphalt, neon signs, and raw concrete. Typography pairs a refined serif for headlines with a clean sans-serif for body text.

Campaign System

Street Culture
